Mr. Miyagi's Was it you?

About

This web page aims to be a memorable platform through which you rethink the way you perceive sexual harassment, regardless of where you come from or what you have witnessed.

We believe in the societal crime of sexual harassment and the need to address it as a systemic issue; it is not one person or a particular club but rather a normalized act that happens more often than we think in our communities, just in front of us.

Even though the setting of the movie and the characters are fictional, similar incidents have occurred countless times in infinite number of places, which include Mr. Miyagi's.

With the hope to destigmatize the people that suffer assault we hope you, dear interactor, to question with us the complexity of consent.
